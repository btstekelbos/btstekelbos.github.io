2. Utilize Triggers: Once you have set up your tags, you can use triggers to specify when they should fire on your website. Triggers can be based on specific events, such as a button click, or on specific conditions, such as a user's location. By using triggers, you can ensure that your tags are only firing when they are supposed to, saving you from unnecessary data collection.
